Home

Dunedin Dairy is a supplier of a variety of dairy products to all New Zealanders. Founded in 1936, Dunedin Dairy began as a simple farm and remains as such to this day.
We have expanded our production capabilities to make a wide variety of dairy products for customers from every walk of life,
whether it is schoolchildren to retired elderlies.
We are very proud of our sustainable farming practices that allows us to produce and sell high quality dairy without polluting the land.
